Go Veg to Save the Planet!

The Problems We are Facing

  1. Greenhouse Gas Emissions
    1. Traps heat and makes the planet warmer
    2. The Agriculture industry contributes to methane emissions that come from livestock, soil, and rice production
  2. Hunger
    1. 70% of grain grown in US feeds livestock, not humans
    2. If more farmland was used for crops for humans, more people could be fed at a lesser expense
  3. Deforestation
    1. Huge areas of land are cleared so livestock can roam
    2. Raising livestock pollutes water and soil
    3. Climate change accelerates

How We Can Help

  • Consume Less Animal Products
    • Cutting back on meat can be hard for some, try Meatless Mondays as a starting point
    • Try soy, almond, coconut, or oat milk as a dairy alternative
    • Use buying power to drive the need for more ethically sourced products




  • Choose Sustainable
    • Veganism isn't just about food, it's about making sustainable choices in everyday life
    • Choose recyclable products and avoid single use plastics
    • Buy from companies that combat the effects of deforestation
